## Grade Band 4–5 (≈ REAL Band C)

§1
**Self-Management.** "Recognize when they are having strong emotions and use different strategies to self-regulate and respond to difficult situations."

§2
**Self-Management.** "Develop short-term goals and create a simple plan that helps them be successful academically and/or personally."

§3
**Self-Awareness.** "Describe how emotions can change depending on the situation."

§4
**Responsible Decision-Making.** "Use a decision-making process to generate both short- and long-term solutions to a challenge or situation."

§5
**Responsible Decision-Making.** "Reflect on personal behaviors and choices and identify what was helpful versus harmful."

---

## Grade Band 6–8 (≈ REAL Band D)

§6
**Self-Management.** "Practice various coping skills to process thoughts and manage stressful situations."

§7
**Self-Management.** "Reflect on progress toward a goal and evaluate the steps needed to achieve this goal."

§8
**Self-Awareness.** "Describe how emotions impact their thoughts, behaviors, and relationships."

§9
**Self-Awareness.** "Identify how interests, strengths, and values influence their academic and social opportunities."

§10
**Responsible Decision-Making.** "Utilize the steps of a decision-making model."

§11
**Responsible Decision-Making.** "Make choices about personal behaviors and social interactions and reflect on how the actions they take impact academic, physical, and/or emotional well-being."

---

## Grade Band 9–10 (≈ REAL Band E)

§12
**Self-Management.** "Examine available support systems and coping skills to identify those that can help process thoughts and manage emotions and stress."

§13
**Self-Management.** "Monitor progress toward a specific goal by developing check-points or adjusting the plan as needed."

§14
**Self-Awareness.** "Recognize a complex range of emotions and describe how events or thoughts can prompt emotions."

§15
**Self-Awareness.** "Reframe a challenge or setback into a positive opportunity."

§16
**Responsible Decision-Making.** "Gather evidence and apply the steps of a decision-making process to support and solve academic, physical, and/or emotional challenges."

§17
**Responsible Decision-Making.** "Use what they have learned from past experiences to help guide future decisions."

## Social Awareness extracts

### Grade Band 4–5

§18
**Social Awareness (verbatim).** "Develop an understanding and appreciation of different abilities, backgrounds, cultures and social groups." "Respectfully understand that others can have different thoughts and feelings." "Understand how stereotypes can be harmful." "Explain the importance of having empathy for others and their perspectives."

### Grade Band 6–8

§19
**Social Awareness (verbatim).** "Respectfully ask questions to learn about the cultures, traditions, and beliefs of others." "Define stereotyping, discrimination, and prejudice and understand how they can be harmful." "Empathize with others' thoughts, perspectives, and emotions."

## Self-Awareness identity-related extracts (lower bands)

### Kindergarten–Grade 1

§20
**Self-Awareness (verbatim).** "List things they like (interests) and things they do well (strengths)." "Practice positive self-talk."

### Grade Band 2–3

§21
**Self-Awareness (verbatim).** "Identify interests and personal strengths inside and outside of the school setting." "Identify personal challenges and advocate for personal needs by asking for help."

## Self-Management — task focus and persistence (Grade Band 6–8)

§22
**Self-Management (verbatim).** "Practice various coping skills to process thoughts and manage stressful situations." "Reframe a challenging situation into a positive learning opportunity for growth."

## Relationship Skills extracts

### Grade Band 4–5

§23
**Relationship Skills (verbatim).** "Demonstrate positive and respectful communication through word choice, and body language." "Practice resolving conflicts with peers independently and/or with adult support." "Approach conflict resolution with an open mind."

### Grade Band 6–8

§24
**Relationship Skills (verbatim).** "Demonstrate positive communication skills, including interactions on social and digital media, and reflect on the potential impact on their reputation and relationships." "Identify the roles of individuals in conflict and activate the steps of a conflict resolution process to manage disagreements and maintain safety and seek support when needed."

### Grade Band 9–10

§25
**Relationship Skills (verbatim).** "Develop strategies for collaborating with others by valuing and encouraging different and diverse perspectives and abilities." "Demonstrate personal responsibility in reaching resolution and identify and access positive support when needed in a conflict situation or crisis (i.e. counselor, trusted adults, security)."

---

## Social Awareness — Grade Band 9–10 (relevant to LT 4.3)

§26
**Social Awareness (verbatim).** "Recognize and describe unfairness and injustice in many forms including attitudes, speech, behaviors, policies, practices, and laws." "Practice empathizing with others' thoughts, perspectives, and emotions, even when they are different from their own."

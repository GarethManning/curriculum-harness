# LT 4.3 — Active Bystander & Anti-Discrimination

REAL learning target description: identify discrimination and targeted harm, assess safe bystander responses, and justify reasoning about when and how to act or seek help. Bands A–F.

---

## Comparison with RSHE

RSHE's secondary respectful-relationships content addresses bullying and stereotyping directly: types of bullying including online, the impact, and "the responsibilities of bystanders to report bullying" [rshe-pilot-extracts §33], plus stereotypes based on sex, race, religion, sexual orientation or disability [rshe-pilot-extracts §35]. REAL's LT 4.3 spans parallel ground at Bands B–E and adds a Band F move RSHE doesn't reach: students analyse a real bystander success or failure and propose conditions that would change the next outcome [real-framework-overview §47].

---

## Comparison with Welsh CfW

WM4 in CfW asks how social influences shape identity — at PS3 learners recognise that "rules, norms and behaviours… have an influence on me" [cfw-pilot-extracts §22], at PS5 "promote positive attitudes and values, and…challenge harmful ones" [cfw-pilot-extracts §24]. Bullying isn't named within Health and Well-being; it appears in the separate RSE Code as one of the harms RSE can help reduce [cfw-rse-statutory-principles §4]. REAL's LT 4.3 focuses on individual recognition and bystander response [real-framework-overview §45, §46]; CfW's WM4 angle is sociological.

---

## Comparison with CASEL

CASEL's Social Awareness at G6–8 names "stereotyping, discrimination, and prejudice and understand how they can be harmful" [casel-pilot-extracts §19]; at G9–10 students "recognise and describe unfairness and injustice in many forms including attitudes, speech, behaviors, policies, practices, and laws" [casel-pilot-extracts §26]. REAL's LT 4.3 shares the recognition content but adds the bystander-response move as a systematic skill: at Band D, justifying a proposed response based on safety, proportionality, and what the targeted person is likely to need [real-framework-overview §46].
